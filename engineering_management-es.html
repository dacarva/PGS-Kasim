ENGINEERING MANAGEMENT TRAINING

Oil & gas . technology . manufacturing

Professionals in Oil & Gas, technology, manufacturing, and construction industries often struggle with striking a balance between technical expertise and management know-how. This occurs because often the core training of engineers and other technical professionals does not include building the competencies needed to effectively lead teams, motivate employees, and cooperate with different types of colleagues. At PROJECT, we have partnered with experts at some of the top business schools in the world to provide training offerings that tackle the most common people problems found in the global engineering workforce today.

The following are a sampling of common workshop topics

Management Essentials
for Engineers

Develop the management competencies of your engineering workforce by giving them the right tools for their leadership toolbox. Focused on first-line managers and new managers, this course provides a better understanding of how to motivate teams, leverage different personalities, and adopt key leadership skills.

Managing
Virtual Teams

Companies with global operations have a particular challenge to manage teams in different countries, time zones, and from different cultures. At PROJECT, we are experts in managing a worldwide workforce. Let us show you the skills needed to bring out the optimum efficiency of your virtual teams.

Global
Leadership

Globalization is changing how companies work as they grow and expand to meet the demands of a worldwide client base. When dealing with differing cultures, people need to evolve in how they manage and lead their workforce. This course provides key skills in understanding others and fostering an inclusive environment.

Managing the Workplace
of the Future

Business trends show that the future workplace will involve connected global teams operating under the same objectives. This course combines key parts of our Managing Virtual Teams and Global Leadership courses to provide a comprehensive training to manage your global business.

Teamwork


Teams are the essential building block of any organization. Unfortunately, they are often fraught with inefficiencies and can be a breeding ground for conflict. This class will help you to build cohesion and psychological safety in your work teams so that you can optimize innovation and reduce common problems such as groupthink and social loafing.

Conflict
Management

Managing conflict effectively is one of the skills that most managers lack. If not managed effectively, conflict can lead to significant detrimental consequences in your organization. This course will provide the skills needed to not only dissipate disagreements amicably but also to leverage respectful debate to increase innovation.

Delivering Effective
Feedback

Prompt and often. Delivering feedback to your employees is a craft that requires both objectivity and finesse in order to be effective. In this interactive workshop, you will learn techniques to provide constructive feedback that will keep your workforce motivated.

Diversity
Management

Today's world is becoming increasingly more global and diverse. With different backgrounds come unique perspectives that can deteriorate into conflict if not managed carefully. This course will give you the tools to not only reduce the distance between diverse individuals, but will also help you to convert heterogeneity into creativity.

Women in
Leadership

In this workshop, you will learn how to advocate the business case of women leaders, recognize and manage common stereotypes of businesswomen, and discover strategies to overcome these obstacles. Key learning points include strategies to help you own your own worth and make yourself visible.
