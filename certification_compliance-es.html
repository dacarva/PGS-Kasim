WHY STANDARDS?


With increasing global standardization and stricter worldwide HSE requirements, regulatory compliance to standards is becoming a difficult and strenuous task for engineering firms. Many companies struggle to develop in-house resources with enough experience to keep-up with the ever-evolving nature of industry standards. At PROJECT, we have developed a team of compliance experts focused on the commonly used standards in the Oil & Gas industry. By outsourcing your compliance tasks to our experts you will be able free up your engineering resources and reduce the time to market for your products.

Additionally, by working with PROJECT, you can be sure that your products will be compliant to regulatory requirements, reducing your exposure to risks and costly non-compliance fees.
