OUR VISION

Project will re-invent engineering services outsourcing by working with globally selected virtual teams that can provide faster turnaround at a lower cost than our comperition

PROJECT was founded by a unique partnership of engineering and business management experts who recognized the limitations of engineering outsourcing to low-cost countries.  We have taken the risk and guesswork out of this process by creating a network of trusted engineering and management freelancers across the globe and by personally managing client-requested projects.  Each of our select freelancers undergoes a rigorous vetting process to ensure the highest levels of quality.  At the completion of each project, our professionals are rated on a 5-point scale and only the top performers remain in our talent pool for future projects.  Whether you need a customized training course or the development of a new product or part, PROJECT can work for you.  We are confident that you will be happy with the final outcome.
